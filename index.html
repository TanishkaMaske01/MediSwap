Our Core Mission

In many rural communities, lack of awareness and infrastructure leads to medicines being thrown in the trash or flushed, harming water sources and soil. MediSwap is the solution.

How MediSwap Works

1

The Request

A community member registers and logs in to signal they have medicine for pickup.

2

Route Optimization

Our logistics team bundles nearby requests to create efficient and timely pickup routes for our specialized vehicles.

3

The Pickup

A trained MediSwap representative collects the medication, provides proper documentation, and ensures safe packaging.

4

Final Disposal

Collected medicines are transported to certified facilities for safe, compliant, and ecological destruction.
